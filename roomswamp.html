Swamp Room

What does a private room booking involve?

It’s the best! When you book a private room at Mox you have a room that is reserved for just you and your friends. You can order anything from our restaurant food and beverage menus and will have a server that will take your orders and take care of all your needs. Make sure to check out the store while you visit, we have a board game library that you can borrow games from while you are visiting free of charge. There are over 400 titles to choose from so there is a game for everyone. Our expert staff will match the perfect game to you and your party after asking some basic questions. You can also bring in your own games. Most of our rooms come with TV’s that you can connect your laptop to and with an Audio hookup so you can play your own music.

How do I book a private room?

You can book any of our private rooms using the Flexbooker portal above. Simply choose the room you are interested in and click “Book Now.” After choosing the date you are interested in, you select the desired start time of your booking. All bookings are 4 hours and you can add more time at this stage if you would like. Next you would fill out the required fields. Once this is completed you will be taken to a secure page to enter your credit card information, a credit card is required to make a booking. We do not charge a deposit at the time of booking, however Flexbooker charges an “authorization fee” of $1, then refunds it to make sure the card is valid at booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation by clicking the link in your confirmation email that says “If you need to change your appointment, please click here.” From there, you can move your reservation to another available time slot, or cancel your reservation altogether. Please note: if you cancel your reservation within 72 hours of your start time, you will be charged a no show fee of $30 for the Red Room and the Green Room or a no show fee of $60 for the Red and Green Rooms Combined.

Can I reserve a private room for less than 4 hours?

You may reserve a room for less than four hours during non-peak times (before 5pm M-F). To do this, create your reservation for the room of your choice with the correct start time. The booking portal will require that you select four hours for your reservation. Just leave a note on your reservation with your desired time frame and we will make the adjustments on our end. You will receive a confirmation email when we have updated your reservation.
